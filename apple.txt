Apples are one of the most popular and nutritious fruits in the world.

Nutritional Value (per 100g):
- Calories: 52
- Carbohydrates: 14g
- Fiber: 2.4g
- Vitamin C: 4.6mg
- Potassium: 107mg

Popular Varieties:
1. Red Delicious - Known for its deep red color and sweet taste
2. Granny Smith - Bright green and tart flavor
3. Fuji - Sweet and crisp, excellent for both eating and baking
4. Honeycrisp - Known for its honey-sweet flavor and crispy texture

Health Benefits:
- Rich in antioxidants
- Supports heart health
- Helps maintain healthy blood sugar levels
- Promotes good gut bacteria
